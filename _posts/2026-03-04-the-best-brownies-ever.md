---

### Ingredients

- ¾ cup cocoa
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- ⅔ cup vegetable oil
- ½ cup boiling water
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1⅓ cups unsifted flour
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- ¼ teaspoon salt

### Instructions

Stir cocoa and baking soda in bowl.
Blend in ⅓ cup oil. Add boiling water; Stir until mixture thickens.
Stir in sugar, eggs and remaining ⅓ cup oil; stir until smooth.
Add flour, vanilla and salt and blend completely.
Pour into lightly greased pan. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 35 to 40 minutes.
Cool. Ice if desired.

### Notes

You can blend in cashew nuts, peanuts or walnuts. You might want to double the recipe and pour into a bigger pan.
